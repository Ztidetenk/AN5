Happy Anniversary,
my love.

Like the tide that always finds its shore, I always find my way back to you.

THINGS I OWE YOU

I'm sorry.
And I mean it.

Some apologies don't need a reason to be earned. They just need to be felt. Here are mine. Not as excuses, but as promises dressed in honesty.

01

For every time I went quiet when you needed my words the most. Silence isn't peace when you're waiting on the other side of it. I know that now.

02

For every message I took too long to reply to. You deserve someone who shows up fast, not someone who makes you wonder if they forgot about you.

03

For the times I might have made you feel like a burden when all you were doing was trusting me with your heart. That trust is the most precious thing you've given me.

04

For anything I said carelessly that landed heavier than I intended. You feel deeply, and I should always remember that before I speak.

05

For not always knowing how to love you out loud. For keeping things in when you deserved to hear them. You are not someone who should ever have to guess how I feel.

06

For everything I don't even remember but you quietly held onto. I'm sorry for those too. The ones you forgave before I even knew to ask.

WHAT YOU ARE TO ME

I love you in
a hundred quiet ways.

This list could go on forever. But here's a piece of it. Every single one of them is true.

  • 01I love how you make me feel like talking to you is the easiest thing in the world. You never make me feel judged. That kind of safety is rare and I don't take it lightly.
  • 02I love the way you care. Not in a performative way, but quietly, genuinely. When you care about something or someone, you really mean it. That kind of heart is not common.
  • 03I love how real you are. You don't pretend to be something you're not, and in a world full of people performing a version of themselves, that realness is something I find myself drawn to constantly.
  • 04I love your mind. The way you think, the things you notice, the way you piece together your thoughts. There is something going on behind your eyes that I could spend a lifetime trying to understand and still want more.
  • 05I love how you feel things deeply. You don't brush things off. You sit with your feelings and that takes a kind of emotional courage most people don't have. I see that in you.
  • 06I love that you're still here. You chose this, chose me, across every inconvenient mile and every timezone. That choice is not small. I think about it more than you know.
  • 07I love how you express yourself. Even through text, even with no voice, you somehow still sound exactly like you. I've learned to hear you through your words alone, and I wouldn't trade that for anything.
  • 08I love your softness. Not weakness, softness. The kind that takes strength to keep. The kind that trusts even when it has every reason not to. That's you.
  • 09I love how you still surprise me. Five months in and you still catch me off guard. I hope that never stops. I hope I never reach a point where I think I've fully figured you out.
  • 10I love that you exist. Genuinely, simply, completely. You are someone the world is better for having in it. And I am someone whose life is better because you chose to be in mine.
A LETTER FOR YOU

My love,

Five months doesn't sound like a lot when you say it out loud. But when I think about everything that five months actually means, every conversation, every late night text, every little thing you've shared with me, every moment I got to spend just knowing you, it feels enormous. It feels like something I'd protect with everything I have.

We don't do calls. We don't see each other's faces light up in real time. We exist entirely through words. And somehow, somehow, you've made that feel like enough. More than enough. You've made texting feel like its own kind of intimacy. Like I can still hear you, even through silence.

I'm in Ireland. You're in India. There's an entire ocean sitting between us and somehow it still doesn't feel like the biggest thing. It just feels like a detail, like something that exists outside of what we actually are to each other. You love the ocean. Maybe that's why it doesn't scare me. Because you're on the other side of it.

I'm sorry for the ways I've fallen short. I'm sorry for every time you deserved more and I gave you less. And I'm not saying that to be poetic. I mean it plainly and simply: I am sorry, and I want to do better, and I will keep trying to.

You make me want to be someone worth missing. Someone worth waiting for texts from. Someone who deserves the specific, quiet, beautiful way you love. I hope I'm getting there. I hope you'll tell me when I'm not.

Happy five months, my love. Here's to every month after this one. Messier, realer, and closer than the last.

Yours, always. 🌊

WHAT I PROMISE YOU

Not just words.
Real promises.

I don't want to just apologize and move on. I want to actually be better. So here is what I am committing to, right now, on five months.

  • 🌊 I promise to never let you wonder how I feel about you. If I love you, you will know. If something is wrong, I will tell you instead of going quiet.
  • 🐚 I promise to show up. Even on the days it's hard, even across the distance, even when I don't have the right words. I will still try.
  • 💙 I promise to never make you feel like a burden for needing me. Your feelings are not inconvenient. You are not too much. You are exactly enough.
  • 🌿 I promise to be more patient. With the distance, with the timing, and with you on the days when things feel heavy for no reason at all.
  • ⭐ I promise to keep choosing you. Not just when it is easy, but when it is 2am and we're both tired and the distance feels longer than usual. Still you. Always you.
  • 🪸 I promise to remind you that you are loved more often than you probably expect. Because you deserve to hear it, not just assume it.

Need a reminder? Press it as many times as you want.
